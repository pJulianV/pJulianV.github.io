TECHNOLOGY | INTERNATIONAL EXPANSION

SaaS Platform Doubles Regional Presence

Client: Enterprise software startup (Colombia)

Challenge: Colombian software management company sought to expand into Peru, Chile and Mexico but lacked knowledge about distribution channels, pricing structure and regulatory requirements in each market.

Solution: We implemented a staggered entry strategy: (1) Market research in each country, (2) Identification of local technology partners, (3) Adaptation of commercial model and pricing, (4) Pilot launch in Peru, followed by Chile and Mexico.

Results:

  • Commercial presence in 3 new countries in 18 months
  • 85% increase in annual recurring revenue (ARR)
  • Network of 12 certified partners across the region
  • 250+ new corporate clients acquired

MANUFACTURING | BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT

Industrial Manufacturer Penetrates Mining Sector

Client: Industrial components manufacturer (Peru)

Challenge: Company with solid presence in general manufacturing needed to diversify into the mining sector but faced entry barriers due to certifications, contacts and unfamiliarity with mining procurement processes.

Solution: (1) Mapping of main mining operators and their procurement processes, (2) Obtaining required certifications, (3) Development of sector-specific business cases, (4) Strategic networking at mining events, (5) Pilot tests with 2 mining companies.

Results:

  • Contracts with 5 mining operations in 24 months
  • USD $3.2M in new revenue from mining sector
  • Positioning as approved supplier
  • 35% operating margin on mining line

RETAIL | STRATEGIC CONSULTING

Retail Chain Optimizes Omnichannel Strategy

Client: Specialty store chain (Ecuador)

Challenge: Traditional retailer with 28 physical stores faced market share loss to digital competition. They needed to integrate e-commerce without cannibalizing physical stores.

Solution: (1) Audit of current operations and competitive analysis, (2) Design of integrated omnichannel strategy, (3) Selection and implementation of e-commerce platform, (4) Team training, (5) Launch of click & collect and ship from store services.

Results:

  • 25% growth in total sales year 1
  • E-commerce representing 18% of mix in 12 months
  • 40% increase in omnichannel average ticket
  • 22% reduction in inventory costs

AGRIBUSINESS | EXPORT

Food Exporter Enters Asian Markets

Client: Superfoods exporter (Peru)

Challenge: Exporting company with strong presence in USA and Europe sought to diversify into Asian markets (China, Japan, Korea) but lacked contacts and knowledge of local food regulations.

Solution: (1) Study of phytosanitary regulations and certifications by country, (2) Participation in trade fairs in Asia, (3) Identification of importers and distributors, (4) Negotiation of pilot contracts, (5) Logistics and documentation management.

Results:

  • Export contracts in 3 Asian countries
  • USD $4.5M in exports to Asia (year 2)
  • Commercial risk diversification 30% Asia
  • 15% premium pricing vs traditional markets

SERVICES | PROJECT MANAGEMENT

Consulting Firm Launches New Service Line

Client: Human resources consulting firm (Chile)

Challenge: Established firm in recruitment and selection wanted to launch organizational consulting services but needed methodology, positioning and specialized team.

Solution: (1) Definition of consulting service portfolio, (2) Development of proprietary methodologies, (3) Recruitment of senior consultants, (4) Go-to-market strategy, (5) Positioning campaign, (6) Management of first 3 pilot projects.

Results:

  • 15 consulting projects completed
  • Net Promoter Score (NPS) of 72
  • 30% margin contribution from new line

FINTECH | COMMERCIAL INTELLIGENCE

Fintech Startup Identifies High-Value Segments

Client: Digital payments platform (Mexico)

Challenge: Fintech with accelerated but unprofitable growth needed to identify higher-value customer segments and optimize customer acquisition costs (CAC).

Solution: (1) Customer base analysis (RFM segmentation), (2) Lifetime value (LTV) modeling by segment, (3) Qualitative research with users, (4) Redesign of focused commercial strategy, (5) Optimization of acquisition channels.

Results:

  • 45% reduction in average CAC
  • 60% increase in LTV of target customers
  • Improvement of LTV:CAC ratio from 2.1 to 4.8
  • Break-even point reached in 8 months
