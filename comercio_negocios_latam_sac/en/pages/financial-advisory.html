Financial Vision

Financial Advisory for Business Development

At C&N LATAM we offer strategic financial advisory specialized in companies seeking to grow, expand or improve profitability. Our approach integrates deep financial analysis with commercial vision, enabling informed decisions that drive sustainable growth.

We work with companies across diverse sectors to optimize their capital structure, evaluate investments, improve cash flow and develop financial strategies aligned with their commercial expansion objectives.

Financial Feasibility Analysis

Comprehensive evaluation of investment, expansion or new business projects. Financial modeling, sensitivity analysis, cash flow projections and determination of expected profitability (IRR, NPV, payback).

Transaction Structuring

Design of optimal financial schemes for mergers, acquisitions, joint ventures and strategic alliances. Evaluation of financing alternatives and structuring of commercial terms.

Working Capital Optimization

Improvement of cash, accounts receivable and inventory management. Strategies to release operating capital, negotiation with suppliers and customers, and optimization of cash conversion cycle.

Business Valuation

Valuation of businesses for M&A processes, partner entry/exit, restructurings or capital raising. Discounted cash flow methodologies, comparable multiples and asset analysis.

Financial Due Diligence

In-depth financial audit for corporate transactions. Review of financial statements, identification of contingencies, quality of earnings analysis and evaluation of financial risks.

Strategic Financial Planning

Development of medium and long-term financial plans aligned with commercial strategy. Budgets, projections, scenarios and investment roadmap to support growth.
