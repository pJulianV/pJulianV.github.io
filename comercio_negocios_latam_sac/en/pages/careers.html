Why CN Latam?

At Comercio y Negocios Latam SAC we offer a dynamic professional environment where talent develops by working on challenging projects with top-tier clients. We seek professionals passionate about commercial development, international expansion and strategic consulting who want to make a real impact on businesses in our region.

What We Offer

Professional Development

Structured career plan with merit-based promotions. Continuous training in consulting methodologies, professional certifications and attendance at international conferences.

Challenging Projects

Exposure to corporate clients across diverse sectors. Work on international expansion strategies, M&A, commercial transformation and high-impact projects.

Competitive Compensation

Above-market salaries, individual and team performance bonuses, premium health benefits and profit-sharing plan.

Flexibility

Hybrid modality with remote work 2-3 days per week. Flexible schedules focused on results. Unlimited vacation policy for senior roles.

Mentorship

Each consultant has an assigned senior mentor. Shadowing program with partners. Quarterly performance reviews and constant feedback.

Collaborative Environment

Open-door culture, multidisciplinary teams, team building events and innovation spaces to share ideas.
