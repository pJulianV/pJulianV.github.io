About Us

Experts in commercial development and business expansion

Who We Are

Our Story

Trade and Business LATAM S.A.C. is a company dedicated to business development, strategic consulting, commercial intelligence, business project management and growth-oriented consulting for organizations expanding in national and international markets.

We offer market analysis services, commercial intermediation, opportunity development, expansion plans and comprehensive support for the creation, consolidation and growth of companies. We are part of the CASNU conglomerate, with a global vision and commitment to excellence.

Mission

Our mission is to develop sustainable business opportunities, providing intelligent strategies, growth tools and high-impact solutions that drive organizations to expand and compete successfully in national and international markets.

Vision

To be recognized as the leading business developer in LATAM, a benchmark in strategy, innovation and business growth, contributing to the positioning and consolidation of the CASNU conglomerate in the region.
